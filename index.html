Abhinav Nellore
I am a computational biology postdoc at Johns Hopkins University working between the labs of Ben Langmead and Jeff Leek. I build tools for integrative analysis of many high-throughput sequencing samples to attack their systematic study on an unprecedented scale. My weapons of choice are in the Hadoop ecosystem, and Rail-RNA is my opening salvo as a project leader.

I was formerly a postdoc with Jun Song at UCSF's Institute for Human Genetics, where I was the principal developer of NSeq, software for identifying positioned nucleosomes from sequencing data. I also contributed to CHANCE, software for quality control of ChIP-seq data, as well as this Cell Stem Cell study of lncRNAs of adult neural stem cells.

I used to to write papers in high-energy physics theory. I also developed this music player sourced by YouTube and the iTunes Store.
